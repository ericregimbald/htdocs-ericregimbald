The Course of Nature

A hunter is tracking his prey. A magazine journalist is chasing her story. Both are after the same thing: a deer. But for very different reasons.

The Course of Nature is a short film that explores the unexpected clash—and possible connection—between two people with opposing worldviews. One sees the deer as tradition, sustenance, and skill. The other sees it as a symbol of compassion, ethics, and life. As their paths cross in the woods, the question emerges: can common ground be found when beliefs run deep?
