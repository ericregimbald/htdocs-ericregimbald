Confessions of a Corporate Clown

A Fringe Comedy about Cubicles, Capitalism, and Clowning Around

Dates: June 20–30, 2025  |  Venue: Calgary Fringe – Venue 5

About the Show

This one-clown show takes you on a hysterical and heartfelt journey from office burnout to circus breakout. Featuring balloon animals, motivational jargon, and a few breakdowns in between.

Run Time: 55 minutes  |  Rating: PG-13

Director's Note

This show is a love letter to the weirdos who never fit in the office—and never stopped trying. We hope you laugh, cry, and maybe reconsider your 9-to-5.
